## Key Trends

- **No-code Democratization**: Platforms like Unsloth Studio are making fine-tuning accessible to non-experts by reducing technical barriers and VRAM requirements
- **Sustainability Focus**: Smaller models enable reduced energy consumption and lower carbon footprints through decreased compute requirements

- **Specialized Use Cases**: The trend toward "appropriate AI" means smaller models handle specific tasks rather than forcing large models into every application
