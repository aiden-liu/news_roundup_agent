# IT News Report — tiny language models

> Generated on 20260402-022017

## Summary

Tiny language models (SLMs) are gaining traction as efficient, cost-effective alternatives to large language models (LLMs). These compact AI systems, ranging from 1 million to 10 billion parameters, enable on-device execution, reduce dependency on cloud infrastructure, and offer faster inference speeds. Key trends include knowledge distillation, architectural optimization, and data-centric training approaches that balance performance with resource efficiency.
